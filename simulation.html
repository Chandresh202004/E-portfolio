What is Motor Imagery?

Motor imagery (MI) is the mental simulation of movement without actual physical execution. EEG signals differ measurably between imagined movements, enabling non-invasive BCI systems.

What is MSVTNet?

MSVTNet is a deep learning architecture combining multi-scale temporal convolutions with a vision transformer, optimised for EEG classification. This project improved BCI accuracy by 15% over the baseline model at NIT Tiruchirappalli.

About This Simulation

This demo simulates the signal processing and classification pipeline. The waveforms, confidence scores, and outputs are generated to reflect real EEG characteristics — not live neural data.
